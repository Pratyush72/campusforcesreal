Help & Support

📌 Overview

At CampusForces, your success is our priority. We are committed to providing fast, responsive, and human-centered support to help students, developers, and learners resolve any platform-related issue efficiently. Whether you're uploading notes, joining live projects, or exploring internships — we are here 24/7.

🛠️ What We Can Help You With

  • Login or Signup issues
  • Note uploads, tag selection & visibility
  • Live Project access and GitHub submission errors
  • Membership upgrades and payment queries
  • Profile editing and resume uploads
  • Missing coin rewards
  • Platform usage and navigation guidance

📬 Support Channels

You can reach us through the following ways:

  • Email: support@CampusForces.in
  • Live Chat (9 AM – 11 PM IST): Bottom-right corner of each page
  • Contact Form: Submit your query
  • Community Forum: Ask the community
  • Docs: Explore platform guides

⏰ 24×7 Support Commitment

We know students often work late. That’s why we offer round-the-clock support through email and forms. Live chat is available daily from 9 AM to 11 PM IST. We promise to respond to urgent queries within 2–6 hours, day or night.

💡 Example Scenarios

  • “Uploaded note but didn't get coins.” → Report via contact form
  • “Live project shows pending after submission.” → Email us with your GitHub link.
  • “Upgraded to Project Pass but still locked.” → Try refresh and chat with us.
  • “Profile picture won't change.” → Try again or contact us for help.
