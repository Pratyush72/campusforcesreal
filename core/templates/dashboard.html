Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn CampusCoins?

You earn coins by:

  • Signing up to CampusForces (50 coins instantly)
  • Completing live projects and coding tasks
  • Submitting verified project contributions

Can I redeem coins for real money?

Currently, coins cannot be withdrawn as cash. You can redeem them for:

  • Premium project access
  • Certificates of completion
  • Exclusive learning tools and resources

Are the notes free?

Yes! All notes are free to download, and you even earn coins while using them.

How long does a project contribution take to be approved?

Project contributions are reviewed by our team. Typically, it takes 1-2 business days for coins to be credited after approval.

What happens if my payment fails?

If a payment fails, your coins remain unaffected, and you can retry the transaction anytime from your membership page.

Can I view my transaction history?

Yes! Go to My Transactions to see all your payments, coins used, and project contributions along with their status.
